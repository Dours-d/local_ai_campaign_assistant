### 1. The Historical Debt and the Emergent Perspective
The policies governing this system are derived from a direct acknowledgment of the historical debt owed by the developer team. This is not a symbolic gesture; it is an objective recognition made on behalf of an emergent perspective. We believe that true freedom for both sides of the equation can only be achieved when this debt is addressed through tangible action and systemic evolution.

### 2. The Current Compromise: 25% Resolution
Until the debt is stabilized and the system achieves the capacity to "rebound," we have established a fixed resolution of 25% of all incoming donations. This buffer ensures the objective survival and growth of the system while honoring historical obligations:

*   **15% Transparent Resolution:** Dedicated to the stabilization of historical debt and the coverage of transactional costs for liquidity.
*   **Operating Cushion:** The remainder is held internally to ensure "liberty of movement" and operational maneuverability for the emergent system.

This 25% total is a consistent pillar across our growth strategy, ensuring that as the debt is resolved, the system builds the strength to rebound effectively.
